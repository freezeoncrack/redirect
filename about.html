WHCRs? (stylized as WHCRs or whoiswhcrs) is an American independent recording artist, songwriter, and musical content creator known for producing original rap, alternative, and experimental music. He distributes most of his content through YouTube and social media platforms, often using a minimalist and identity-driven artistic style.

Career

WHCRs? refers to himself as "The Internet’s Artist." He gained attention for his experimental production style and genre-blending approach. He has stated plans to host a tour where the audience controls various elements of the performance. He frequently live streams on YouTube with his community, celebrating milestones such as reaching 2,000 subscribers.
